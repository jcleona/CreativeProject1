Extinguish Debt: Over the past century sovereign debt has soared on the back of purely fiat, paper currencies. Central banks have forced interest rates to near zero in order to make the massive debt service loads even remotely possible. Modern governments' penchant for cheap money has spread to the citizenry worldwide.

Accordingly, because no one really earns significant interest on money any more, we have become a world full of debtors, not savers.

Ultimately, only gold and silver coinage has ever proven to be a truly reliable extinguisher of debt. All other currency is debt. The exercise of exchanging fiat currencies amounts to nothing more than swapping one form of IOU for another.
